## Joyce's *Elements* and related tutorials

The textual narrative, diagrams, proposition layout, page structure,
and accompanying images in the following directories are
**&copy; David E. Joyce**, used by his permission:

- [`elements/`](elements/) &mdash; Euclid's *Elements*, Books I&ndash;XIII
- [`geomlib/compass/`](geomlib/compass/) &mdash; Compass Geometry tutorial
- [`geomlib/round/`](geomlib/round/) &mdash; spherical / inversive geometry tutorials
- [`geomlib/eulerline.html`](geomlib/eulerline.html) &mdash; Euler line demo

Dr. Joyce wrote these between 1996 and 2020 as part of his online
edition of the *Elements* and supplementary materials, originally
hosted at Clark University. His permission to republish this content
on `euclids-elements.org` (and to port the underlying applet to
TypeScript as `geomlib`) was granted to Nelson Brown in May 2026.
For the exact wording of his permission grant, see the
[NOTICE](https://github.com/brownnrl/euclid/blob/main/NOTICE.md) in
the `geomlib` source repository.

**Third parties wishing to redistribute Dr. Joyce's narrative content
should obtain separate permission from him.** The permission granted
to Nelson Brown was specific; it is not transitive.
